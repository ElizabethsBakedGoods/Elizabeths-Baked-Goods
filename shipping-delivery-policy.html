Delivery & Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Local Delivery

We offer delivery to the Houston area, including Richmond, Rosenberg, Katy, and Sugar Land. Delivery fees vary based on distance.

Delivery Window

Customers will receive a delivery window once the order is confirmed.

Pickup

Customers may choose free local pickup at a scheduled time.

Shipping Within the United States

We offer shipping throughout the United States for select baked goods. Items that contain frosting will be shipped with the frosting packaged separately to maintain freshness and prevent melting during transit.

All shipped orders are carefully packed to ensure safe delivery. Processing and shipping times vary depending on destination and order size.

Failed Deliveries

If no one is available to receive the order at the scheduled time, the customer may need to reschedule delivery for an additional fee.
