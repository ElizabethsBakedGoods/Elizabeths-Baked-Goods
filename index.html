About Elizabeth

Hi! I'm Elizabeth, the baker behind all of these sweet creations. What started as a few holiday cookie boxes for friends and family turned into a full-time passion. I love experimenting with recipes, designing seasonal boxes, and creating treats that make celebrations extra special.

Every order is handmade in small batches using high-quality ingredients — nothing mass-produced, nothing rushed. From rich, buttery cookies to delicate macarons, each dessert is carefully crafted to bring joy to your table.
