Return & Refund Policy

Perishable Goods

Due to the nature of baked goods and food items, we do not accept returns of perishable products. If there is a quality issue, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery or pickup.

Custom Orders

Custom items are made to order and are non-refundable once preparation has begun. We’ll do our best to make it right if there’s an issue—please reach out promptly.

Order Changes & Cancellations

For local orders, changes or cancellations must be requested at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled delivery/pickup time. For online orders, please contact us immediately; changes may not be possible after checkout.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 24 hours with photos and your order details. We’ll evaluate and offer a replacement or partial refund where appropriate.
