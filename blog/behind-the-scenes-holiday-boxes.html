Behind the Scenes: How I Make My Holiday Boxes

Published December 2025 | By Elizabeth

My holiday treat boxes are one of the most requested items I make every year. They're festive, packed with a variety of treats, and carefully designed to make a beautiful gift. But creating them is a process that takes planning, organization, and creativity. Today, I'm taking you behind the scenes.

Step 1: Designing the Theme

Every holiday season starts with a vision. I pick a color palette, choose packaging materials, and plan the treat combinations. Whether it's classic red-and-green or something modern and neutral, the theme sets the tone for the entire box.

Step 2: Baking in Batches

Holiday boxes include cookies, brownies, macarons, candy, and sometimes specialty treats. I bake each item in batches based on what stays fresh longest. Harder treats come first, delicate items last.

Step 3: Packaging Each Item

Individually wrapping each treat protects the textures and prevents flavors from mixing. I use cellophane bags, parchment wraps, or mini bakery cups depending on the item.

Step 4: Assembling the Boxes

Heavier treats always go on the bottom, while softer or more delicate items sit on top. Decorative filler keeps everything secure and adds a festive touch. I always add a handwritten tag—it's a small detail that customers love.

Step 5: The Final Touches

Ribbon, stickers, and seasonal decorations give each box a polished finish. Every box feels like a special gift, not just a collection of treats.

Reflection

Making holiday boxes is time-consuming, but seeing customers light up when they receive them makes it all worth it. Every box is made with care, and the joy they bring is priceless.
