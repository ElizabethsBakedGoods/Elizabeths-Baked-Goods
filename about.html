About Elizabeth’s Baked Goods

Fresh ingredients. Festive packaging. Made with care.

My Story

Elizabeth’s Baked Goods began as a small home-based project in Rosenberg, Texas. Over time, friends, family, and neighbors encouraged me to share my treats with others — and what started as holiday gift baskets turned into a full seasonal baking business.

I focus on fresh ingredients, high-quality presentation, and traditional homemade flavors. Whether you’re ordering for a teacher, coworker, holiday gift, or event, each treat is made with care.

What Matters to Me

  • Consistency in quality
  • Simple, clean ingredients
  • Fun, festive packaging
  • Affordable homemade treats for families

Thank you for supporting my small business. I look forward to baking for you!
