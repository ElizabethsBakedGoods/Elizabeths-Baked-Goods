Kid-Friendly Hot Cocoa Mix Recipe

Creamy, Mild, and Perfect for Kids

This homemade hot cocoa mix is creamy, mild, and perfect for kids. Made with evaporated milk powder, cacao powder, and sugar, it creates a smooth, comforting cup of hot chocolate using just hot water. This recipe is easy to make, budget-friendly, and ideal for family use, gifting, or small-batch selling.

Unlike darker, more intense hot chocolate recipes, this cocoa mix is intentionally lightly chocolatey and similar in sweetness to store-bought versions, making it a great choice for children. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use regular cocoa powder instead of cacao powder?

Yes. Regular unsweetened cocoa powder works well and will produce a slightly milder flavor. The results are nearly identical. Both are made from cocoa beans, so feel free to use whichever you have on hand.

Does this hot cocoa mix need milk to prepare?

No. The evaporated milk powder allows this mix to be prepared with hot water only, which makes it convenient and kid-friendly. However, adding fresh milk creates a creamier, richer cup if preferred.

Is this safe for kids?

Yes. This recipe contains no caffeine-heavy chocolate and is formulated to be gentle and kid-approved. The ingredient list is simple and recognizable. Always supervise young children with hot liquids.

Can I use this recipe for small-scale selling?

This recipe is suitable for personal use, gifting, and small-scale cottage food preparation. However, regulations vary by location. Check your local cottage food laws before selling homemade food products.

How long does the dry mix stay fresh?

Properly stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry place, this hot cocoa mix will stay fresh for up to 3 months. The evaporated milk powder can absorb moisture over time, so ensure your container seals tightly.

Can I make this recipe in larger batches?

Absolutely. This recipe scales easily. Just maintain the same proportions: 4 parts evaporated milk powder, 1 part cacao powder, 2 parts sugar, and a pinch of salt. Mix and store the same way.

Storage & Shelf Life

Store the dry mix in an airtight container in a cool, dry area. Properly stored, it will stay fresh for up to 3 months.
