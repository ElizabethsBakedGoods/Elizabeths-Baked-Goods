Shipping & Delivery Policy

Local Delivery

We offer local delivery in Richmond and Rosenberg, TX and select nearby areas. Delivery windows are scheduled during checkout or confirmed for local orders. A flat delivery fee may apply.

Pickup

Pickup is available by appointment. Please use the Local Ordering form to schedule a pickup time and location.

Processing Times

Orders are typically prepared within 24–72 hours depending on volume and item type. Custom orders may require additional time.

Shipping

We do not ship perishable items outside of our local delivery area. Non-perishable items may be shipped when available; shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

Missed Delivery

If a delivery attempt is missed, we’ll attempt to reschedule once. Additional fees may apply for repeated attempts.

Questions? Please use our Contact page.
